The Beginning of the End

The Beginning of the End 
2023
Video
Adobe Audition, Premiere Pro
Filmed using iphone 12 Pro Camera and Canon EOS Rebel T7i,
audio captured on iphone.

For this project I made a video based on a soundscape that I had made. It was essentially using the reverse process of normal video production. The sounds were recorded on my phone then altered and compiled in Adobe Audition. I distorted the sounds to make them sound “creepy” and “supernatural”. After I had finished the soundscape, I recorded footage of my sister and myself. I wanted a creepy, paranormal feel from this video to match the sounds. When I was recording the footage, I used very dramatic lighting and high contrast. The video and the audio were compiled together in Adobe Premiere Pro. I utilized a lot of effects and masks in order to isolate the desired subjects from the video.
