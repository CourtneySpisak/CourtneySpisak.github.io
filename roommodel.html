Room Model

Room Model   
2022
Maya for the model, Photoshop for the UVs

This is a 3D model of a room that I made in Maya. It is based on the concept art by Stephanie Lee. I modeled, UV mapped, and textured the entire room. All of the texture mapping was done in Photoshop. The final model is rendered using the Arnold renderer.
