About the Artist

Courtney Spisak

Truck Bomb

TruckBomb  
2023
Game, Pixel art Sprites
Aseprite 

This is a game I contributed to with the members of my group from the Game Development Club at San Jose State University. It received the award for “Most Promising Game” at the SJSU Game Development Club Spring 2023 Awards. I made all the sprites for the trucks, cars, buildings, and trees. In addition, I also made all of the art for the mini games. All of the sprites were drawn in Aseprite.
